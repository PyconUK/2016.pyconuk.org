Open Day


Our day of introductory talks and workshops


Thursday 15th September

For the first time, PyCon UK will begin with an Open Day: a whole day of of activities especially for the benefit of newcomers to Python.

Our Open Day is Thursday and will be held at Cardiff University Bute Building, King Edward VII Avenue, Cardiff CF10 3NB

Entrance is free and open to everyone, but you'll need to tell us you're coming by obtaining a ticket below, even if you've already got a ticket for the main conference.

Visit our ticket page on Tito if you don’t see a ticket form.
